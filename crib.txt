abcm2ps eastneuk.abc --topmargin 300pt -e 126 -s 1 --transpose 2

   Above sets the top margin at 300 points = 4.something inches,  so
that it could be printed as the second tune on a page.

  --transpose 2 went up two semitones from G to A.
